# Program Overview

## What This Program Is

This 3-day AI training program is a delivery system that moves from shared context to real workflow adoption. It starts with the company brain, then trains role-based cohorts, then leaves each participant with a working AI employee setup instead of a pile of notes.

## Goals Of The 3-Day Intensive

- Build a usable Company Brain from real stakeholder knowledge
- Create role-specific workflow examples and artifacts
- Set up a practical AI employee for each participant
- Leave the client with a clean handoff and next-step path

## Delivery Sequence

1. Capture and polish the six core Company Brain documents
2. Train the cohorts on foundations, tool choice, and role workflows
3. Configure AI employees with instructions, context, and tested tasks

## Expected Outputs

- Six approved Company Brain documents
- Cohort-specific workflow artifacts
- Session recordings and training materials
- AI employee instructions, loaded context, and test logs
- Final handoff bundle with QA notes and next steps

## Client Responsibilities

- Provide access to key stakeholders and existing materials
- Review and approve Company Brain documents
- Confirm attendee roster, session logistics, and key contacts
- Share open questions or blockers early

## Team Responsibilities

- Keep extraction separate from training
- Keep examples tied to the client's real work
- Make QA visible before anything moves to delivered
- Maintain a cleaner client-facing layer than the internal ops layer
