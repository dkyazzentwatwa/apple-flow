# 3-Day AI Training Hub

This workspace is the central delivery hub for the 3-day AI training program. It contains the Company Brain materials, cohort training resources, AI employee setup assets, delivery status, QA checkpoints, and final handoff materials. Use the navigation below to move by phase, resource type, or delivery status.

## Program Overview

The delivery sequence stays in this order:

1. Company Brain Build
2. Role-Based Cohort Training
3. AI Employee Setup

This workspace is designed to keep internal ops and client-facing materials separate while still giving the client one clean place to review approved assets, recordings, guides, and final handoff resources.
